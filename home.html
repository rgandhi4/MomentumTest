Welcome to Momentum!

The aim of this tool is to help your school work organized, whether you are in high school or college.



How It Works:

Begin by inputting an upcoming assignment in the calendar, on the date it is due.

Go to the Assignments tab to see the task you just added on this page.

Use the dropdown to select the corresponding course for your assignment.

Update your progress on your assignments in the Progress tab.

The QuickList tab can be used to jot down small tasks that can be completed quickly.

View the Resources page to find helpful tools to aid you in your schoolwork.
